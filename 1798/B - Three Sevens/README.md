On day $$$i$$$, $$$n_i$$$ people with the numbers $$$a_{i, 1}, \ldots, a_{i, n_i}$$$ participated in the lottery.</p><p>It is known that in each of the $$$m$$$ days, only one winner was selected from the lottery participants. The lottery winner on day $$$i$$$ was not allowed to participate in the lottery in the days from $$$i+1$$$ to $$$m$$$.</p><p>Unfortunately, the information about the lottery winners has been lost. You need to find any possible list of lottery winners on days from $$$1$$$ to $$$m$$$ or determine that no solution exists.</p></div><div class="input-specification"><div class="section-title">Input</div><p>Each test contains multiple test cases.
